Data Deletion Request

Your Right to Data Deletion

At Chess Tournament Manager, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. In accordance with data protection regulations, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal information from our systems.

When you request data deletion, we will remove all personal information associated with your account, including but not limited to:

  • Your profile information (name, email, contact details)
  • Your account credentials
  • Your tournament history and participation records
  • Any content you have created or uploaded
  • Authentication data from third-party services (Google, Facebook)

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your data in one of the following ways:

Option 1: Use the Data Deletion Form

Complete the form below to submit your data deletion request. We will process your request and send a confirmation email once completed.

Option 2: Contact Us Directly

You can also email your data deletion request directly to our Data Protection Officer at:

Email: privacy@chesstournamentmanager.com

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Any additional information that might help us identify your account

What Happens After Your Request

Once we receive your data deletion request:

  1. We will verify your identity to ensure the request is legitimate.
  2. We will process your request within 30 days.
  3. We will send you a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.
  4. Any data that we are legally required to retain (such as transaction records for tax purposes) will be retained only for the legally mandated period.

Please note that once your data is deleted, your account will be permanently closed, and you will no longer be able to access our services using that account.

Third-Party Authentication Services

If you used Google or Facebook to sign in to our service, we will delete the data we have stored about you in our systems. However, this does not affect the data that Google or Facebook may have collected about your use of our service.

To manage or delete data held by these third-party services:

  • Google: Visit Google's Data & Privacy page
  • Facebook: Visit Facebook's Privacy Center
