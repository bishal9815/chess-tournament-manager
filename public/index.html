Why Choose Our Platform?

Powerful features designed for tournament organizers and players

Automated Swiss Pairings

Our system automatically generates fair pairings using the Swiss system, saving you time and ensuring balanced matches.

Real-time Standings

Track tournament progress with live standings, tiebreaks, and statistics updated in real-time as results are entered.

Player Management

Easily register players, import from Chess.com, and manage participant information all in one place.

Create a tournament in minutes with our intuitive setup wizard. No technical knowledge required.

Advanced Tiebreaks

Multiple tiebreak systems including Buchholz, Sonneborn-Berger, and more for accurate final standings.

Mobile Friendly

Access your tournament from any device. Perfect for updating results on the go during events.
