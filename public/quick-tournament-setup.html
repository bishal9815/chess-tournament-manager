Step 1: Tournament Details

Swiss System: Players meet opponents with similar scores.
Double Swiss: Two Swiss tournaments with combined standings.
Round-Robin: Each participant plays against every other participant.
Knockout: Players are eliminated after losing a match.
Scheveningen: Team-based competition where each player faces every opponent from the opposing team.
Monrad (Danish): Modified Swiss allowing leaders to face trailing players.
Random: Opponents assigned randomly without considering scores.
Accelerated: Modified Swiss designed to speed up top-tier matchups.
